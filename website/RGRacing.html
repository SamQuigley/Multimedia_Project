When the games were released

April 1st

Kill Them all

Kill Them All is a top down shooter game, This game was designed for the player just to have fun. The aim of the game is too kill as many monster as you can before you die. You have five chances before you die and your score resets to zero.

April 3rd

PONG

This is an remake of a old school classic. This is a easy to learn game for anyone! the game is designed for two people too play. One player will control a paddle with up and down key and the other paddle controlled by the w and s key.

April 7th

RG Racing

RG racing is a fun racing game, with easy contrls. The car is controlled by the arrow keys on the keyboard. This game was easily created inside of the construct 2 engine. A game like this can be created using behaviours such as 9 direction and the car behaviour. You can set the blocks to solid to craete colisions between the car and the wall.
